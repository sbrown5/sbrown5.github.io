Cyclistic bike share

The purpose of the case study is to analyze historical ride data to impact business tasks. The historical data will reveal the what and when but not the why. Cyclistic is fictional bicycle share company in Chicago. The company currently features 5,824 bicycles and 692 docking stations. Cyclistic sets itself apart by also offering reclining bikes, hand tricycles, and cargo bikes, making bike-share more inclusive to people with disabilities and riders who can’t use a standard two-wheeled bike.
